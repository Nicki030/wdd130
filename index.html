Del Rio

Del Rio, Texas is that it has a cool museum called the Whitehead Memorial Museum. This museum has lots of old stuff from the history of the area. It's named after a famous local guy named John N. Whitehead, who loved collecting things. When you visit, you can learn all about the interesting history and culture of Del Rio and the nearby places. It's like taking a trip back in time!
